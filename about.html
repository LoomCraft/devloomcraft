Our story

We create every occasion essentials that blend comfort, durability, and a mindful design approach. Based in India, we partner with responsible manufacturers and source materials with care.

"LOOM CRAFT GIFTS" specializes in eco-friendly return gifts that blend traditional craftsmanship with sustainable materials. Our mission is to promote conscious gifting by offering products that are handmade, biodegradable, and rooted in nature. Each item is thoughtfully designed to reflect elegance while caring for the environment. We craft unique gifts using River Grass, Date Palm, Water Reed Grass, Palm Leaf, Banana Fiber, and Bamboo, ensuring every piece is a testament to sustainability and artistry. Perfect for weddings, corporate events, and special occasions, our gifts are not just presents—they are a statement of responsibility toward the planet.
